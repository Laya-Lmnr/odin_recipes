Cupcakes

Description

Cupcakes are delightful little treats that bring the joy of cake into a perfectly portioned, handheld form. Soft, fluffy, and topped with creamy frosting, they’re ideal for birthdays, celebrations, or simply satisfying a sweet craving. Whether you prefer classic vanilla, rich chocolate, or creative flavors like red velvet or lemon zest, cupcakes are easy to bake, fun to decorate, and always a crowd favorite.

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven and prepare the cupcake tray with liners.
  2. Mix dry ingredients in one bowl and wet ingredients in another.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients until smooth.
  4. Pour batter into cupcake liners, filling each about 2/3 full.
  5. Bake for the recommended time, then cool on a wire rack.
  6. Frost and decorate as desired.
