Description

Chickpea Salad is a light, nutritious, and satisfying dish that’s perfect for any meal. Packed with protein-rich chickpeas, fresh vegetables, and a zesty dressing, it offers a burst of flavor with every bite. Quick to prepare and versatile, this salad is ideal for a healthy lunch, a side dish, or a refreshing snack.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas (or ~1.5 cups cooked chickpeas), drained and rinsed
  • 1 medium cucumber, diced
  • 1 medium tomato, diced
  • 1 small red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup bell pepper (any color), diced
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley or cilantro, chopped
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese (optional)
  • 2‐3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1‐2 tbsp lemon juice (freshly squeezed)
  • 1 tsp cumin powder (optional, for extra flavor)
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 tsp red chili flakes (optional, for a little heat)

Steps

  1. Drain and rinse chickpeas, then place them in a large bowl.
  2. Dice cucumber, tomato, and bell pepper; finely chop red onion and add to the bowl.
  3. Chop parsley or cilantro and sprinkle over the salad; add feta cheese if using.
  4. Drizzle olive oil and lemon juice; season with cumin, salt, pepper, and chili flakes if desired.
  5. Toss everything gently until well combined.
  6. Serve immediately or chill for 15–20 minutes for best flavor.
