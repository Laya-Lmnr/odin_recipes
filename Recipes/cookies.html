Choco Chip cookies

Home

Description

Chocolate Chip Cookies are the ultimate comfort treat : crisp on the edges, soft and chewy in the center, and bursting with melty chocolate in every bite. Made with simple ingredients like butter, sugar, and vanilla, these cookies fill your kitchen with a rich, buttery aroma that’s impossible to resist. Whether enjoyed warm from the oven, paired with a glass of milk, or tucked into a lunchbox, chocolate chip cookies never fail to bring a little sweetness and nostalgia to any day.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Cream butter and sugars together until smooth.
  3. Beat in eggs and vanilla extract.
  4. Mix in flour, baking soda, and salt; then fold in chocolate chips.
  5. Scoop dough onto the baking sheet and bake until golden.
  6. Cool on a wire rack before serving.
