Red Sauce Pasta

Home

Description

Red Sauce Pasta is a classic Italian-inspired dish that combines perfectly cooked pasta with a rich, tangy tomato-based sauce. Infused with garlic, herbs, and a hint of spice, this comforting dish brings together the vibrant flavors of ripe tomatoes and aromatic seasonings. Whether topped with melted cheese or served plain with a drizzle of olive oil, red sauce pasta is a simple yet satisfying meal — perfect for busy weeknights or cozy dinners at home.

Ingredients

  • 200 g pasta of your choice
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2‐3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 medium bell pepper, diced (optional)
  • 400 g tomato puree (or crushed tomatoes)
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • ½ tsp red chili flakes (optional)
  • Grated cheese or parmesan, for topping (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  2. Sauté garlic, onions, and other vegetables in olive oil.
  3. Add tomato puree, herbs, and seasonings; simmer until thickened.
  4. Toss the cooked pasta in the sauce until well coated.
  5. Add cheese or garnish if desired.
  6. Serve hot.
